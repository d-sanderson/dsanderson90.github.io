Weekly Reflections

Passion Project

I started teaching myself how to play Piano 4 years ago. I've always been a musical person but told myself that it was too late in my life to learn an instrument. When my desire to create finally overcame my doubts, I got on craigslist and bought a basic keyboard. I started teaching myself and practicing for an hour each day. I really wanted to be able to play my favorites songs from Zelda and Donkey Kong and some classical music that I had heard growing up with my Dad. I made a lot of progress and often would go over my scheduled hour of practice just because I was enjoying myself. Eventually I bought a sweet Yamaha Electronic Keyboard that had weighted keys and a beautiful sound. When I practiced for over an hour, I would enter a focused state and all of my worries and doubts would sort of fade away. I was brought into a pleasant flow state where I could be creative and relaxed. This drove me to continue my practice.
